## Seasonal Patterns

### Peak Months by Market

| Market | Primary Peak | Secondary Peak | Quiet Period |
|--------|-------------|----------------|--------------|
| US | Sep–Oct | Jul–Aug, Nov–Dec | Apr–Jun |
| UK | Sep–Oct | Mar, May | Jan–Feb |
| AU | Oct | Jun–Aug | Jan–Mar |
| NZ | Aug–Sep | May–Jul | Jan–Apr |
| CA | Jan–Feb | Mar | Apr–Dec |

**Key insight:** CA is inverse to the other markets — peak in Jan–Feb suggests fiscal year budgets being spent and early Q1 trade shows. US/UK/AU/NZ all peak in the Sep–Oct trade show season.

**Proactive outreach timing:**
- For US/UK clients: Reach out in **July** to book Sep–Oct orders
- For AU/NZ clients: Reach out in **August** for Oct rush
- For CA clients: Reach out in **November/December** to catch Jan–Feb budget spend

## Discount Rate Context

Discounts were applied on ~44–59% of all orders. This is normal BDS behavior.

| Rate | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| 44–50% | Standard market behavior (US, UK, AU, NZ) |
| 59% (CA) | Reflects higher negotiation rate for larger B2B deals |

When a client asks for a discount:
- Small repeat order: 7% is standard, up to 10% before escalating
- Volume 200+ units: 40% (fast) or 60% (extended) — confirm with production
- CA clients negotiating: More flexibility expected, but confirm with manager above 10%

## Average Order Value Benchmarks

Use these when a quote seems off or to set client expectations:

- **US avg:** ~$943 — typical single media wall + accessories order
- **AU avg:** ~$1,021 — slightly larger average, often includes photography products
- **NZ avg:** ~$998 — similar to AU
- **CA avg:** ~$1,334 — larger orders, higher-value products (lit displays)
- **UK avg:** ~$533 — shorter runs, more individual/small business buyers

Orders above $5,000: high-value — consider offering complimentary mockup proactively and personal follow-up from Jennifer.
