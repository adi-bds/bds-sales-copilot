# BDS Customization Rules & Calculations

**Source:** Internal training doc — "Customization and calculations - basics"

---

## Products That CANNOT Be Customized

These are sold as-is — do not offer custom printing or sizing:

1. **Roll-up banners**
2. **Retractables** — all retractable products
3. **Popup Velcro displays**
4. **SEG Popup displays**
5. **Slim fabric LED back-lit displays** — the back panel is gray by default and cannot be printed on. This is by design to contain the light within the system.

---

## What BDS Does and Doesn't Do

**We DO NOT offer:**
- Vinyl printing
- Brochure printing

**We CAN do:**
- Replacement fabrics for frames not purchased from us — always get the frame details before quoting
- Interlocking and foam floor carpets — but these are all pre-designed; contact Idris for these requests
- Double-sided banner printing → refer to **Duplex Fabric Printing** product
- Custom finishings: pole pocket size, zipper colors, velcro color (white or black), eyelet size & color, fabric ties, zip ties, etc.

**Canopy billboard:** Always ask the client for the cross-section shape of the poles before quoting.

## Popup Display — Customization Conditions

The popup frame is a **3x3 grid** where each cell is approximately **2.5ft x 2.5ft**.

- Dimensions must be in **multiples of 2.5ft** (both width and height)
- **Recommended max:** 12.5ft H x 25ft W
- **Over 25ft wide:** Combine multiple frames side by side
  - Example: Client wants 50ft → place two 25ft frames adjacently = 50ft display
- **Pricing:** Calculate from the closest available standard dimensions

---

## Straight Tension Media Wall — Customization Conditions

Applies to both **curved** and **straight corner** versions.

- **Any dimensions are possible**
- **Recommended max height:** 10ft — if client insists, can go up to 12ft
- **Width up to 25ft:** No issue, single piece
- **Width up to 30ft:** Possible as single piece, but always advise client that 2 x banners is better beyond 25ft
- **Width over 30ft:** Must use combination of multiple banners
- **Note:** As width increases, additional support feet are required in the middle
- **Pricing:** Calculate from the nearest standard dimensions

## SEG Lightbox — Customization Conditions

Two frame profiles based on height:

| Height | Profile Thickness |
|--------|------------------|
| Up to 6.5ft H | 80mm |
| 6.5ft H and above | 120mm |
| Client requests 120mm for smaller frames | Can accommodate |

**Key specs:**
- Power supply is external; cable exits from **left side bottom**
- LED modules are embedded on the **left and right sides** running through the full height of the frame
- **Max customizable height:** 10ft H
- **Width conditions:** Same rules as straight tension walls (see above)
- Can do **single-side fabric** (no back fabric) — but warn the client: if the back is exposed to external light, illumination intensity will be reduced. Fine if placed against a wall.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT — Pricing:** SEG Lightbox custom pricing is calculated based on **HEIGHT** (because LEDs run along the height). Never quote custom SEG pricing yourself — always confirm with **Idris, Kaviya, Lavanya, or Areefa**.

## Straight Tension Media Wall — Fabric Add-On Pricing

For fabric-only customization (no frame):

- Any size can be produced
- **Sizes up to 30ft x 20ft:** Use standard catalog pricing
- **Sizes above 30ft x 20ft:** Must calculate from final price — escalate to manager

**Known reference price:**
- 20ft x 30ft fabric = **$1,078**
