## Delivery Times (as communicated to clients on calls)

- **Standard delivery: 5–7 business days** (mentioned 397 times — this is the go-to answer)
- Express/Rush: 4–5 business days (additional cost, subject to team confirmation)
- International / longer orders: 10–12 business days
- Minimum possible with express: 4–5 business days — never promise less

**Key phrase used by reps:** "We need around five to seven business days to deliver and that time includes production, shipment, and delivery — you don't have to worry about anything separately."

## Replacement Fabrics (266 mentions)

One of the most common inquiry types — clients who already have frames and only need new graphics.

**What reps say:**
- "Can you please confirm — are you looking for the fabric with frame or just the fabric?"
- "We can do just the fabric. Only the fabric, okay. We do have the fabric with frame that will also look good, but if you're looking at only the fabric, I'll share the quote."
- Always ask for exact frame dimensions before quoting — even a slight size difference causes fitting issues
- Replacement fabrics are available for frames not purchased from BDS — just need the exact specs

## Double-Sided / Duplex Printing (62+ mentions)

Clients frequently ask whether backdrops can be printed on both sides.

- **Yes, we can do double-sided** — refer to the Duplex Fabric Printing product
- Pricing is roughly double the single-sided equivalent
- Clarify whether the client needs two different artworks or the same artwork mirrored
- For media walls, clarify if both sides are customer-facing or just one

## Pole Pockets (62 mentions)

Common customization request for fabric banners and backdrops.

- Standard sizes: 3-inch pole pocket is most common; also available in 4-inch and 6-inch
- Can do pole pockets on top and bottom, or all four sides
- For large widths (20ft+), eyelets are preferred over pole pockets — reps advise: *"For 20 feet, I wouldn't suggest plastic hooks — too many needed. Eyelets or pole pockets work better but eyelets are probably best for that width."*
- Can do envelope corners (45° angle openings) for pipe-and-drape setups — confirm with production

## Wrinkle-Free / Fabric Quality (231 mentions)

Clients consistently ask whether fabrics arrive wrinkle-free.

- **Standard rep answer:** Our fabric is wrinkle-free / non-wrinkle. If any minor creases from shipping occur, steam iron on mild setting resolves it.
- TrueKolor eco-friendly fabric is specifically marketed as "eco-friendly, wrinkle-free"
- If a client reports wrinkles: "You can use a steam iron and that should sort it out — we've shared care instructions in the email as well."

## Outdoor Use (158+ mentions)

Frequent question: can BDS products be used outdoors?

**Key facts to communicate:**
- Fabric backdrops can be used outdoors with a standing frame, but are **not fully weatherproof**
- Fabric will absorb light water/moisture but artwork remains visible
- **No lamination** available on fabrics — no vinyl options
- Not suitable for heavy rain or prolonged outdoor exposure
- Canopies and outdoor tents are purpose-built for outdoor use
- For menu boards / permanent outdoor signage: BDS products are not ideal — vinyl would be needed (which BDS does not offer)

**Rep example:** *"We just do fabric backdrops, so it will absorb a little water as it's fabric — but it can stand outside with a frame. It can't stand heavy weather though."*

## Discounts & Bulk Pricing (1,563 mentions)

One of the most common client asks.

**Standard rep approach:**
- Individual product discounts: escalate to manager/team lead — reps don't quote discounts directly
- Bulk/reseller pricing: "I can't decide anything on this — I'll pass this to my manager and she can have a call with you and discuss."
- Adding items to an existing order to get a better rate: "Yes, you can do that — reply to the same email thread and request to add the item."
- Repeat customer discounts: handled case-by-case by management

## Rush Order Handling

Rush orders are extremely common — handled as follows:

1. Check if express shipping can be added (confirm with shipping team)
2. Minimum lead time even with express: **4–5 business days**
3. If order is already placed: "Let me check with my shipping team if we can express this — if it's already in production we may not be able to change it, but I'll find out."
4. Never promise delivery dates without confirming with the team first
5. For event-critical deadlines: take the event date, work backward, advise clearly if it's achievable

**Common rep language:** *"So it's like five to seven business days to deliver and that includes production, shipment, and delivery — everything. If you need it sooner, we do have an express option at four to five business days that I can check on for you."*
